Women in Science (WiSci)

WiSci is an initiative that empowers young women in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics by providing access to hands-on STEM experiences and networking opportunities. It fosters leadership and collaborative skills for young women, preparing them for a future in STEM fields. The program includes workshops, mentorships, and summer camps for high school students.

Association for Women in Science

AWIS is one of the leading organizations dedicated to advancing women in STEM fields. It offers mentorship, professional development opportunities, networking events, and advocacy for policies that support gender equality in science and engineering. Girls Who Code (GWC)

Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization that works to close the gender gap in technology by teaching young girls coding skills. The organization runs after-school clubs, summer immersion programs, and provides resources for learning programming and computer science in a fun and supportive environment. It's aimed at creating a pipeline of future women tech leaders.

STEMinist

STEMinist is a network dedicated to creating a supportive community for women in STEM across the globe. Through the STEMinist platform, members can access mentoring, webinars, job opportunities, and discussion forums about challenges facing women in scientific careers. The platform also highlights inspirational stories of successful women in various STEM disciplines.

National Girls Collaborative Project (NGCP)

The NGCP seeks to bring together organizations that are committed to encouraging girls to pursue careers in STEM. It provides a wealth of resources for educators, mentors, and students, including funding opportunities, collaborative projects, and training materials. The goal is to create a diverse workforce by inspiring and empowering the next generation of female leaders in STEM.

Women in Engineering ProActive Network (WEPAN)

WEPAN is a national organization that provides resources for women in engineering and works to advance the professional development of women in the field. They offer programs that focus on gender equity, provide tools for mentorship and career development, and advocate for systemic changes in academic and workplace environments.
